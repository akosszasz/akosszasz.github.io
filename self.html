PERSONAL IDENTITY
-

This is the presentation of my personal identity.
During the design process I had a few things in mind. I wanted to use geometrical shapes instead of typography, however as a wire I still used my monogram (see below). With this logo I wanted to represent my work, my experiences and my interests.
I also wanted to make this shape into a physical object emphasising the architectural inspiration. This 'installation' is overall 262,5x100x6mm, made out of thin plywood painted black.
