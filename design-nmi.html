About Neticle Media Intelligence

Neticle Media Intelligence is the flagship product of Neticle, it's a Machine Learning-driven media-monitoring platform turning textual data into measurable information based on keywords and semantic phrases.

Feature Highlights

Modular Chart System

In NMI, the system has three views regarding data visualisation. These are the report view, text feed view and the insight view.
